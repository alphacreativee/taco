TACO DEL SOL

A story that began in 2020 on the bustling streets of Phu My, where our little taco stand became a nightly gathering place. People lined up, eager to taste our authentic Mexican flavors, made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of passion.

Then, like many businesses, we were forced to close our doors during the pandemic. But the love for Taco del Sol didn’t fade. Our loyal customers kept asking, “When are you coming back?” And so, we listened. We’re back, bigger and better than ever, now perched on a rooftop in the heart of Saigon, offering a vibrant escape from the city below.
